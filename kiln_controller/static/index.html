Autotune Power Settings
Preheat Cruise Multiplier (default 1.0)
1.0× ⌨
Scales the output % used during the preheat cruise phase — the steady climb to setpoint before relay oscillation starts. If your oven stalls or climbs too slowly during autotune preheat, increase this. Try 1.5 or 2.0 for underpowered ovens. Has no effect on the relay oscillation phase.
Relay High Multiplier (default 1.0)
1.0× ⌨
Scales the output % used during the relay oscillation phase of autotune — the bang-bang cycling that measures your oven's response. If the oven can't swing above setpoint during autotune, increase this. Too high causes large temperature swings and less accurate results. Try 1.5 for weak ovens.
Temperature Calibration
Offset (°F) — add or subtract from all readings
0.0°F ⌨
Boiling water = 212°F. If reading 213.6°F, set offset to -1.6°F.
